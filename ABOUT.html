ABOUT

IZZI LEE

Born in Seoul, South Korea but I consider Los Angeles to now be home. I moved over when I was 16, it was a mess. I was lucky to be such a mess, I had nothing to lose so tried everything. From being a video editor, graphic designer to photographer, I became capable of wearing many hats.
While in South Korea I trained heavily on traditional fundementals of art and design. Though it was helpful, the focus on pure accuracy limited my perception of art. It took many years but I learned the importance of play and fun in creativity. In 2023 I started making cartoons about the joys, pains, and mundane of my ordinary life. I will forever be grateful for all the people it reached and continues to reach.
